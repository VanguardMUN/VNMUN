Lakkshay Dhanuka

Moderator

"I am a problem solver, a trouble maker at Mun's and a change maker" ~ Lakkshay Dhanuka. He is an Aspirant International MUNner with completion of around 30 Mun's , an International debater, an artist, and an organizer.

He loves to explore the world through different lenses, and believes that everyone has a voice that deserves to be heard. He is passionate about using his skills to create a more just and equitable world. Since the start the only thing he has ever looked forward to is the heated debates and controversy in every competition he took part in.

VNMUN is a space for aspiring speakers and debaters to exercise their talents in the field of Debate, Research, Current life events and Leadership.
