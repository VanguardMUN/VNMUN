Secretariat

Vividh Masilamani

An established orator and a decorated MUN exponent, Vividh is a renowned figure in the realm of MUN. A zealous participant in more than a dozen MUN’s, he has to his credit, 2 best delegations, multiple high commendations and has even chaired 3 editions of MUN.

Paving his way to the third round in ISDS is no mean feat and comes as no surprise that he has bagged the first place in all major inter house competitions. Vividh is excited to embark on another major life journey as he gears up to serve as Secretary General of the very first edition of VNMUN’24.

VNMUN is a space for aspiring speakers and debaters to exercise their talents in the field of Debate, Research, Current life events and Leadership.
