About The Event

Vanguard Model United Nations 2024 was initially an initiative by Vividh Masilamani (Chettinad Vidyashram Alumni, batch of 2024) and Nidha Khan (Bhavanâ€™s Rajaji Vidyashram, Batch of 2026) but has quickly grown into a multifaceted project with multiple hands helping and multiple talented individuals pooling in their expertise to create what now exists as VNMUN.
VNMUN is a space for aspiring speakers and debaters to exercise their talents in the field of Debate, Research, Current life events and Leadership. By being a conference headed by well known, coveted individuals who have years of experience in the field of Model UN and Debate, VNMUN promises an experience that will leave attendees wanting to dip their hands into and solve some real world problems.
