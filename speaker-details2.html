Secretariat

Nidha Khan

Nidha Khan is an extremely ambitious 15 year old who started exploring her many talents in her freshman year of school.

Since then, she has attended multiple MUNs, winning awards in most of them, has started her own Literary Magazine, has served as an editor of multiple magazines, landed multiple high profile internships and now adds ‘Founder and Organizer of Vanguard Mun’ to her list of achievements.

She’s a gifted speaker and writer who wishes to one day become a lawyer, who can take pride in the quest for justice in the darkest realms of life.

VNMUN is a space for aspiring speakers and debaters to exercise their talents in the field of Debate, Research, Current life events and Leadership.
