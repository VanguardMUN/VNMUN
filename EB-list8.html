Sudharsan Sukumar

Chairperson

Sudharsan Sukumar is a second-year Chemical Engineering student at SSN College of Engineering on weekdays and an MUNner on weekends. He is considered a jack of all trades but master of none. His knowledge base on International Relations and diplomacy stems from his past experiences in Model United Nations conferences. Known as a 'Right of Reply' delegate, he is prone to being aggressive when the situation demands it in the committee. Specializing in Human Rights, he firmly believes that with thorough research and impactful actions, delegate can emerge victorious.

Poojitha

Vice Chairperson

Poojitha is an ardent orator who had multiple accolades in MUN's and debates. She has a 100% award streak with awards in every MUN she's been to. She's easily progressed through levels of ISDS and is a gifted speaker and writer. She aspires to go above and beyond and explore the unknown of what's above. After chairing 2 editions of MUN with more to come she looks forward to chairing and hopes for an engaging committee.

Vihaan Reddy

Co Vice Chairperson

Meet Vihaan Reddy, who can be best described as a dedicated and well-rounded individual who considers MUNning a hobby. As a formidable ISDS debater, an exceptional orator, and a calculated chess player, he stands tall and apt for this post. According to him, MUNing requires 3 qualities: the passion to understand the nuances of geopolitics, hard work, and the perseverance to strive hard to succeed.

VNMUN is a space for aspiring speakers and debaters to exercise their talents in the field of Debate, Research, Current life events and Leadership.
