Vaideesh

Chairperson

Meet the Chairperson of UNHRC, Vaideesh, who certainly finds it difficult to track his experience in MUNning, having attended more than 40 MUNs. With varied interests, he excels in every field that he steps into. As an individual who is more fascinated by dealing with numbers, he enjoys taking up the job of trading and managing portfolios. Apart from catering to learning music, we can catch Vaideesh binge watching his favourite series in his me- time. He also adds, "Do not fear failure but rather fear not trying." As he looks forward towards chairing the event, he expects the delegates to be confident while also being diplomatic and tactful at attending the issues.

Nidha Khan

Co Chairperson

Nidha khan is a 15 year old who dreams of one day becoming a lawyer that will change her own small part of the world. She holds immense pride in being the Co chair of VNMUN’s UNHRC committee and can't wait to see the conference unfold.

Sowmiya Narayanan

Co Chairperson

Sowmiya Narayanan, a first year student has always been a person drawn towards MUN. With a fair share of MUN experiences and counting in his bag he encourages good and efficient debate in the committee.

"Why do we fall, Bruce? So that we can pick ourselves up" is what he would recommend to all the delegates to not give up on MUN based on their awards but rather use it as a learning curve.

Apart from MUNs his favourite hobby is to binge watch Christopher Nolan's movies for his mind boggling themes explored and is always up for a challenge to do adventurous activities outdoors.
