Jada Adarrsh

Chairperson

Jada Adarrsh, an experienced and accomplished journalist, excels in the ever-evolving realms of journalism and film. His enthusiasm for the profession is evident as he shares his expertise with aspiring reporters in MUNS, nurturing the upcoming generation of storytellers and artists.

Recognized for his exceptional filmmaking and editorial lifestyle that captivate audiences and mind-blowing interviews that leave audiences shaken, Jada also demonstrates prowess on the soccer field, establishing himself as a proficient goalkeeper. Beyond his journalistic pursuits and athletic endeavors, he channels his creativity into filmmaking, editing, and content creation, cultivating a strong presence on social media with over 100k views, and showcasing a diverse and vibrant passion for the arts.

Riddhima Sakrani

Vice Chairperson

Riddhima Sakhrani, is an ardent writer and a passionate reporter, who started her MUN journey not so long back, but has been a published author and poet ever since she was 13 and had just discovered her zeal for all things creative.

Being the deputy editor for a literary magazine, an intern at a law firm in chennai, a sportsperson for various sports and even being a part of her school sport teams, a classical and contemporary dancer for more than half her age and more; Riddhima would now like to add to her plethora of things she doesn't really claim to be achievements but rather endeavours of her curiosity and ambition for life, the title of the Vice Chairperson of IPC +IPP and the Head of Logistics at VNMUN'24 with great honour and pride.

She looks forward to seeing such a great entity such as VNMUN unfold in front of her eyes as a culmination of the hard work and enthusiasm put in by the OC, EB and participants alike.
