Sanjay Senthilnathan

Chairperson

As an international debater, national skater, and state cricketer, Rishabh embodies a relentless pursuit of excellence.

With a remarkable record of participating in over 15 Model United Nations (MUNs) without ever experiencing defeat.

Rishabh firmly believes that MUNs are a gateway to expanding one's perspectives and acquiring skills that hold long-term value. Guided by the mantra, "One person can make all the difference" by John F. Kennedy.

Sundar Ganesh S

Co Chairperson

Sundar Ganesh is an enthusiastic eleventh grader from CVA who is deeply immersed in the realm of Model United Nations (MUN).

Commencing this journey in sixth grade, Sundar has ardently participated in 35+ conferences, securing awards in almost all of them, being in the EB multiple times. Fueled by a passion for diplomacy and global issue resolution, he consistently finds himself drawn to the captivating world of MUNS.

He is also a keen observer of Indian Politics. Sundar always looks forward to the interplay of diverse ideas and the quest for consensus amidst differing perspectives.
