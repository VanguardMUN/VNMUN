Rishabh Jain

Co Chairperson

As an international debater, national skater, and state cricketer, Rishabh embodies a relentless pursuit of excellence. With a remarkable record of participating in over 15 Model United Nations (MUNs) without ever experiencing defeat. Rishabh firmly believes that MUNs are a gateway to expanding one's perspectives and acquiring skills that hold long-term value. Guided by the mantra, "One person can make all the difference" by John F. Kennedy.

Sajith Rayhan

Co Chairperson

As a member of the Executive Board, it is our pleasure to introduce Sajith Rayhan, a distinguished delegate with over 20 MUNs to his credit, showcasing his expertise and dedication in socio-economic and analytical committees. Rayhan's commitment is unwavering, driven by a genuine passion for meaningful dialogue and problem-solving. He believes that "Success is not final, failure is not fatal: It is the courage to continue that counts." This quote encapsulates Rayhan's resilient spirit and his belief in the importance of perseverance. His approach embodies determination and resilience, motivating others to persist in the face of challenges. Rayhan anticipates a conference filled with insightful discussions, innovative solutions, and a collective dedication to excellence

Raya Althaf

Moderator

As a member of the Executive Board, it is our pleasure to introduce Raya Althaf. Raya brings life to cultural events, filling them with energy and a mix of talents. People are drawn to her lively presence and infectious enthusiasm. As a debater, she grabs attention, smoothly making persuasive arguments. However it's not just her talking skills that make her special, but rather her curious nature and knack for finding the truth that set her apart from the rest. She's always ahead, digging deep to uncover important facts. In everything she does, Raya leaves a lasting impression. She believes in the saying, "Someone else's success is not your failure," which shapes her positive attitude and encourages support among peers.
